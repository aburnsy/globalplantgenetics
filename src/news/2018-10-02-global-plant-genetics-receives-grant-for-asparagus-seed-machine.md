We are delighted to report that our application for grant funding to help support our purchase of an asparagus seed counting machine has been approved. It was an application through the New Anglia LEP’s Small Grant Scheme funded by the ERDF in Norfolk and Suffolk. The purchase of this new machine will allow us to increase efficiency as we develop sales of asparagus seeds across the globe.
