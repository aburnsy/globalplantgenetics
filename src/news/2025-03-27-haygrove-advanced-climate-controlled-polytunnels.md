Haygrove manufactures polytunnels and substrate gutter systems for growers cultivating over 30 different crops across more than 50 countries. Beyond equipment design, the company operates its own farms in the UK, South Africa, Portugal, and China, producing berries and cherries. This dual role as both manufacturer and cultivator provides insight into industry challenges including climate management, geographic variations, crop-specific requirements, labor availability, and technological advancement.

The company's polytunnel technology has progressed significantly over the past decade. Their flagship innovation, Total Vent, features "a fully retractable roof, providing unparalleled polytunnel climate management." Propagators in the UK and Australia report excellent results using Total Vent structures, while Jordan-based growers utilize Haygrove's EZvent tunnels with mechanized venting for strawberry propagation.

## Key Benefits

Automated climate control enables "frequent, quick, and precise" adjustments that maintain healthy growing conditions while reducing labor demands. The mechanized systems regulate temperature, humidity, light, and airflow consistently. Closing tunnels during rain protects plants while enabling rainwater collection for irrigation purposes.

Compared to traditional glasshouse propagation, Haygrove's structures deliver comparable climate precision at substantially lower capital costs, making advanced horticultural technology more financially accessible.
