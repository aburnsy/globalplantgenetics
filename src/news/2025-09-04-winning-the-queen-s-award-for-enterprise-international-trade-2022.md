Winning the Queen’s Award for Enterprise: International Trade 2022 was a huge milestone and moment to remember, for Global Plant Genetics in our first decade of business.

The Queen's Awards for Enterprise – the UK's highest accolade for business success.

With (at the time) 74% of our turnover coming from overseas and clients in 33 countries, we were and now more so, recognised as a leader in international business.
But it is not just about sales – it is about values, governance, people, and community.

This award belongs to our whole team – staff, clients, breeders, partners, and all wider stakeholders.
It gave us a major boost and will be something we continue to look back upon as we continue to grow, expand our IP portfolio, and attract top talent.

To everyone who supports us – thank you.

World-class genetics. Global partnerships.
The journey continues…
