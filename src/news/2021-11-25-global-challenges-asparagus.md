Much like in many other parts of the fresh produce and other industries, the asparagus business has been undergoing significant change in the recent past. So, two years on from the start of the Covid pandemic, what is the current state of the global asparagus market? What are the key challenges for growers and others in the supply chain? What’s next for one of the world’s most premium vegetables? In this piece, we will be looking at the prevalent factors affecting key asparagus players within our sphere.
