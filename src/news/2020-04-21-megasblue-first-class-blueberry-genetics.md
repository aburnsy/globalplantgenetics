MegasBlue represents a significant advancement in blueberry cultivation, developed through decades of dedicated breeding work. The variety was created by Bob Gabriel, proprietor of Oregon Blueberry Farms and Nursery in the USA, who operates both a substantial commercial production operation and a respected plant nursery.

Gabriel initiated his blueberry breeding initiative during the early 2000s. After two decades of refinement, his program has produced varieties that compete with leading international breeding efforts. MegasBlue emerged as one of the initial releases from this comprehensive development pipeline.

## Key Characteristics

The variety demonstrates several desirable agronomic traits. It requires approximately 1000 chill units annually and exhibits vigorous growth patterns. The fruit displays distinctive light blue coloring without the reddish pigmentation sometimes seen at harvest time. Additionally, the berries maintain a favorable equilibrium between sugar content and acidity, making them suitable for both direct consumption and mechanical harvesting operations.

## Market Potential

"This high yielding variety is set to replace many traditional varieties where improvements in yield, flavour and size are important," according to the announcement. The variety addresses commercial needs across Northern Highbush production regions intended for fresh markets and processing facilities.

Licensed propagators are currently scaling up plant production to meet anticipated demand, with advance ordering recommended due to expected strong interest.
