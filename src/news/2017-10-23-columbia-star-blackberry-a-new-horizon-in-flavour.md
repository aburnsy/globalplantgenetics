Columbia Star is a new blackberry variety, bred at Oregon State University, USA. It is especially known for its wonderful flavour, meaning that consumers just want to keep eating it. This particular flavour aspect is founded on a combination of high sugars and low acids. The balance of these components delivers an eating experience that makes Columbia Star a true dessert blackberry.
