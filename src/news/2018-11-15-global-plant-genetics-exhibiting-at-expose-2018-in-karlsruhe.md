Next week, we will be making our annual journey to the German town of Karlsruhe in order to exhibit at the ever-popular ExpoSE trade show. The exhibition is focused on asparagus and berry growing and has been expanding annually ever since its inception; this is now the 23rd edition and 460 exhibitors from 14 countries will be in attendance.
