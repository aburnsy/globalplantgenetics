We’re now back from Fruit Logistica 2018 in Berlin. Once more, we were impressed by the sheer scale of the event. Some statistics from 2017 show just how important it is for the fresh produce industry across the world. More than 75000 visitors attended the show from 130 countries to view exhibits from over 3000 companies. It truly is an international event.
