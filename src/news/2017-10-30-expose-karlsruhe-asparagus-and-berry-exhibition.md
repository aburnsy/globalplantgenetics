We are pleased to announce that once again this year, we will be exhibiting at the prestigious asparagus and berry show, ExpoSE, held in Karlsruhe, Germany. The event takes place over two days, 22-23 November and sees many of the leading industry players both attending and exhibiting. We had a great reaction to our presence last year and met with many new, as well as existing industry partners.
