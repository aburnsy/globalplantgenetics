This is the final part of our blog series on asparagus production in New Zealand. It is already three months since we were in the country. Their harvest season is well over now and crops in the Northern Hemisphere are just starting to be cut. This just goes to show the value of the extensive work that has been put into the asparagus crop in order to make it a product that we can buy 12 months of the year.
