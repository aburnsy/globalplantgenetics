Global Plant Genetics and FD Berries have finalized a blueberry licensing and supply chain partnership focused on the Indian market. Under this agreement, FD Berries will cultivate and license premium evergreen and low-chill blueberry varieties to contracted growers, leveraging their investment in state-of-the-art tissue culture and nursery infrastructure.

## Key Perspectives

Rupert Hargreaves of Global Plant Genetics expressed optimism about market transformation, stating: "Working with Sam Ahilan and his team on this project has been a pleasure and our combined strengths will be a real asset."

Sam Ahilan, representing FD Berries, emphasized the value proposition: "The new blueberry varieties offer everything we as growers and shippers are looking for in terms of productivity, size, firmness, shelf life and flavour."
