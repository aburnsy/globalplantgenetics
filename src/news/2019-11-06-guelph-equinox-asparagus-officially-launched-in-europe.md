Global Plant Genetics has just announced the official launch of the Guelph Equinox asparagus variety in Europe. This took place during a presentation given by Jamie Petchell of GPG and Ken Wall of Fox Seeds, Canada at the International Asparagus Days event in Angers, France. The launch is the culmination of many years of in-depth trials and the first small-scale test plots across a range of territories.
