Intellectual Property & Licensing Services

Comprehensive IP management and licensing solutions for elite plant genetics worldwide

Our Role

Global Plant Genetics serves as the trusted IP manager and licensing partner for world-leading plant breeders. We bridge the gap between breeders' innovations and commercial growers, ensuring elite genetics reach global markets while protecting breeder rights and maximizing value.

Services We Offer

🌱

Variety Sourcing & Licensing

Access to elite genetics from world-leading breeding programs including UC Davis and other premier institutions. We negotiate licensing agreements that benefit both breeders and growers.

🛡️

IP Protection & Enforcement

Comprehensive intellectual property protection through plant patents, PBR (Plant Breeders' Rights), and trademark registration. Rigorous stewardship and enforcement of breeder innovations globally.

🤝

Breeding Partnerships

Strategic partnerships with elite breeding programs worldwide. We facilitate collaboration, funding, and commercialization pathways for breakthrough varieties.

🌍

Global Market Representation

Worldwide breeder representation across multiple continents and growing regions. We connect breeders with growers, nurseries, and distributors in key markets.

🔬

Technical Agronomic Support

Expert guidance on variety selection, growing practices, and production optimization. We support licensees with technical resources and agronomic expertise.

📊

Market Intelligence

Global market insights, trend analysis, and competitive intelligence. We help breeders and growers make informed decisions based on market dynamics.

Our flagship partnership with UC Davis provides access to world-class strawberry and grape genetics. UC Davis breeding programs are renowned for innovation in berry flavor, disease resistance, and rootstock development.

Beyond UC Davis, we represent and collaborate with leading breeding programs across berries, asparagus, and tree nuts. Our global network ensures access to cutting-edge genetics adapted for diverse growing regions.

How Licensing Works

1

Initial Inquiry

Contact our team to discuss your crop interests, growing region, and market goals. We'll identify varieties that match your requirements.

2

Trial Evaluation

Conduct variety trials in your growing conditions. We provide technical support and agronomic guidance throughout the evaluation period.

3

License Agreement

Negotiate license terms including territory, royalty structure, and production volume. We ensure fair agreements that benefit all parties.

4

Ongoing Support

Receive continuous technical support, market updates, and access to new releases. We're partners in your success throughout the license period.
